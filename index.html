Vision and Mission

Vision:

Our motto is to enunciate scrupulous vision in generating the desire for ethical values and venerate in cultivating a culture of excellence through the development and implementation of personalized learning plans that set students on the right path to attain glory.

Mission:

  • To imbibe the value of humility and diligence in the minds of students.
  • To nurture our prodigies in developing their social skills in converting every adversity into opportunity for progress.
  • Inculcating spiritual awareness in young minds to develop discipline and reverence towards nation.

Our Approach

PATRIOTISM:

To celebrate and honour the courage of National Heroes and Freedom Fighters to inculcate Patriotism.

LOVE OF GOD:

We conduct daily prayers, weekly Bhajans and religious classes apart from celebrating religious festivals visits to temples, discourses etc. lead to love of God.

INTELLECTUAL DEVELOPMENT:

A high standard of curriculum, faculty wise teaching, common valuation of common examinations, enquiry method of education, frequent quiz programmes, encouragement of spirit of competition, ensure the intelletual development.

TALENT DEVELOPMENT :

Competitions in every field of human innovation,special vocational training and culturals etc. showcase the special talents.

PERSONALITY DEVELOPMENT :

To imbibe the values and also to be confident and competent, personality development camps are organised for the students.

Rules and Regulations:

  1. Students Handbook must be brought to the school daily.
  2. Students must be in the School premises at least 15 minutes before the first bell. Late comers should bring a letter informing / indicating the reason for the delay or be accompanied by their parents to explain the reason for late coming and also give an assurance that the late coming will be not repeated.
  3. It is compulsory for every student to come in proper school uniform.
  4. Students must be neat and tidy.
  5. Students are responsible for the safety of their books and belongings. They are advised not to bring any valuable articles to school. School will not be responsible for the loss of any property of the pupil.
  6. Students are strictly forbidden to purchase food articles from vendors near the school premises.
  7. Students must take part in all extra curricular activities of the school as a part of civic training.
  8. All the students must take part in school physical training and sports activities unless declared physically unfit or exempted by the Principal.
  9. Students suffering from contagious or infectious disease shall not be allowed to attend the school till the quarantine period , to ensure speedy recovery of the child
  10. No private tuition by our school teacher will be arranged or encouraged.
  11. Parents should address their letters only to the Principal.
  12. Parents and guardians will not be allowed to meet their children during class hours.
  13. Parents are requested to cooperate with the school in enforcing Regularity, Punctuality and Discipline in the interest of progress of their children . They should check the students Hand Book daily and take note of home work given and see that it is done before they are sent to school the next day.
  14. A record of addresses of the parents or guardians is maintained in the school office. Any change of address should be communicated without delay.
  15. Parents and guardians are requested to send their children to school with hair properly combed. Boys should have hair cuts at regular intervals.

Winning Moment of CBSE Exhibition 2019

Winning Moment of CBSE Exhibition - 2019

 

We are proud to announce that our school students A.R.Harshavardhan of X-G and S.Aditya of X-I won the National level science Exhibition conducted by C.B.S.E. at Mayoor school , Noida on 8th and 9th Feb 2019 based on the theme – scientific solutions for challenges in life focusing to reduce destruction caused by lightning. 470 teams participated all over India. Our team has been shortlisted as one among the 21 teams and the participants were felicitated in the grand valedictory ceremony.

Students from our school exhibited the comparison of surface areas different shapes eg. Cone, Hemisphere, Sphere, Frustum, combination of Hemisphere, Frustum and Cylinder. By comparing the above said shapes students structured the Mathematical Model–

Zeus evader made of copper which has the maximum surface area for accumulation of charges.

They proved that the probability of lightning capture is maximum for the proposed model. It is more economical and effective than the current existing lightning arresters which will reduce the destruction caused by lightning.

 


 

 

Facilities and Infrastructure

School Buildings


Classrooms

Well-ventilated,airy, bright and spacious classrooms giving a cordial atmosphere for learning.


Labs

Physics Lab

Theory is incomplete without practicals. To broaden and deepen the inquisitive learning of students , a well-equipped physics lab is set up to ensure that every student strengthens his / her knowledge of theory with lively practicals.


Chemistry Lab

To provide a dynamic learning environment that is student centered and curriculum driven, a well-equipped chemistry lab with worktables that has a sink, water facility to clean, natural gas connection for heating, and a safety shield. Safety equipment includes a fire extinguisher and a first-aid kit.


Biology Lab

In the field of learning environment, students work individually to use the processes and materials of science to construct their own explanation of biological phenomena. The lab activities of students are centered with their active participation and they engage in hands on, minds on activities using laboratory material and techniques.


Computer Lab

The computer lab in our school empowers young people to compete in the global area and pursue careers across all sectors. We have nearly 80 computers with all amenities to work in that room. Students are provided a convenient seating place to excel in programming and for doing practicals.


Library

A resourceful library is setup in our school to foster, support and stimulate the enjoyment of reading and to develop the recreational intercepts of students. “A book never runs out of battery”. The more you read, the more things you know. We inculcate reading habit among children to grope for an information and to create an excellent hobby. Our school library has been instrumental in acquainting the students with facets of Literature and Knowledge to excel in all their accomplishments.


KG Room

Well-ventilated, airy spacious classrooms giving a cordial atmosphere for learning .LED televisions are installed in all the Pre K.G class rooms to motivate the children to learn with fun and also to visualise the rhymes and stories with audio effect.


Play Area

A play area for our K. G. Children is set up to offer an early learning programme through the intervention of planned play activities that enable children to develop physical co-ordination, strength and flexibility in children to grow and reach their potential. Our school has a big play area and has a specific design to play indoor games. We have a few recreation equipments such as climbers, slides, merry go round, mazes and swing set etc. Students recreate and enjoy the timewhere the team spirit is also strengthened.


Park

Park is desinged in such a way to enable the students to go ahead with fun and frolic with their age group. Children actively play on the swings,slides and see-saws.Personal safety is also ensured in this area.


Auditorium

Our school has a big auditorium which could hold 700 students. We gather here to show case our skills, hear a speech,sing bhajan songs and hymns on all special days. We conduct open day meetings.We gather in the auditorium for the orientation camps, meetings with the Principals and also for a few important occasions.


AV Room

"Audio visual education is an instruction where particular attention is paid to the audio and visual presentation of the Material”.The students’ confidence is honed in a suitable and conducive environment grooming the future leaders of our nation. The Audio Visual room is equipped with Multimedia projector and Digital Cameras. Students will get multimedia experience based on the topic and they will be interested to learn more and more as visual aids kindle their brain to work efficiently with the goal of improving comprehension and retention.

POLICE FLAG DAY - 02-11-2020

Greater Chennai Police had observed POLICE SERVICES FLAG DAY during October 21.10.2020 to 31.10.2020 to highlight the martyrdom of Police personnel who sacrificed their life while on service to the nation.

In consonance to the above,an Elocution competition was conducted among schools on the topic”Role of Police in Nation Building” in English through online due to the current pandemic situation.

Hence participation from various schools across the city was invited through online and the same was registered through the given mail id.

D.A.V SCHOOL, ADAMBAKKAM had registered for two entries in English and Tamil respectively.

P.JAGATHPATHY PRANAV OF XI-A  had participated in English Elocution..The first round was on 27th October 2020.The Judge was the Alandur Assistant Police Commisioner.There were about 20 participants from the neighbouring schools.Pranav was selected among the three participants shortlisted in the Zonal level.(SOUTH)

The next round was city level, which was held on November 2nd 2020.Here around 24 participants across the city had participated which was really a challenging one. The competition was broadcasted live from the office of Police Commissioner ,Chennai.

All the 24 shortlisted candidates were honoured with a “Certificate of Participation” and the top three students were awarded a trophy.                                                         .

71st REPUBLIC DAY CELEBRATIONS - 26-01-2020

71st REPUBLIC DAY CELEBRATIONS

The 71st Republic Day Celebration at D.A.V.School was a jubilant function reminiscing the founding fathers of the Indian Constitution. Like every year, it was a grand fanfare of cultural events, drills, band display and patriotic speeches and most important of all the March Past by the students of V std. The event, anchored by students of XI std. Preetish Narayan & Yogini, was presided by Chief Guest, Shri Rajesh Ramaswamy, V.P. COGNIZANT Technology Solutions. In his speech, he appreciated the show put up by the students of D.A.V.School and rendered an inspiring speech asking students to strive for success and achieve great heights in their lives. He was all in appreciation of the speeches delivered by the students of grade X in English, Tamil and Hindi. The Principal Smt. J.Geetha , in her speech welcoming the Chief Guest, emphasized on the remarkable constitution given by its architects. The welcome dance by the girls of the school, was a fusion of various dance forms of Tamil Nadu. The students of II std. enchanted the audience with their Mass Drill using fans as property. The Mass Drill combining Aerobics presented by the boys of VIII std not only set everyone’s feet tapping but also cast their spell with wands as property. The band display by the students was at par with ‘Beating the Retreat’ at Rashtrapati Bhavan. Prizes were distributed by the Chief Guest to the winners of various sports events held for students, teachers as well as the non-teaching staff during the year. The show concluded with Vote of thanks delivered by the Vice Principal Smt. K.V.Pramila followed by the national anthem.
